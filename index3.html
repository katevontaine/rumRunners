RUM RUNNERS

Celebrating the Spirits & Flavors of the West Indies


Rum Runners brings together the hottest global rum distilleries with Charleston's top chefs to create one-of-a-kind paired cocktails & dishes.

Saturday, June 25 at 6:00pm | Memminger Auditorium


The Producers of the sold-out Bacon and Bourbon invite you to join them in exploring the spirits and flavors of the West Indies with 10 chefs and 30 rum distilleries serving up delicious pairings. Pay homage to the spirit that gave the Rum Runner its name, pirates, and all the best flavors of the West Indies.
